Welcome to my portfolio.

Hello! My name is Josh Slebodnik, and I'm an iOS Engineer from Fargo, ND. With over 16 years of programming experience, my journey began primarily as a web developer from 2008 to 2014. However, in 2014, I transitioned my focus towards mobile app development, delving into both iOS and Android platforms initially. Over the past four years, I've dedicated my efforts solely to iOS development, honing my skills and expertise in crafting exceptional mobile experiences. I also strive for 100% code coverage and a clean app architeture.

Having freelanced for the past few months, I'm now eager to pursue a full-time opportunity. Below, you'll find a compilation of projects I've undertaken for various companies throughout my career.
